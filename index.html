Intro

Hi! My name is Sam and I am a software engineer from Brazil. I have always enjoyed problem solving and the learning of new technical skills. This year I have enhanced my skills on Javascript, Ruby on Rails, React/Redux, CSS, HTML, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS and much more. I completed three projects in total, a 2D game using vanilla JavaScript and canvas, an amazon clone project using rails as the backend, react/redux as frontend, and PostgreSQL as database, and a group project using the MERN stack.

Outside coding, I have a diversified background growing up in Brazil and now living in the States. I am passionate about education, philosophy and psychology and I want to use technology to make these things available to students in the third world who don’t have English as their first language.

You can reach out to me at sam.martins.code@gmail.com

Euphrates

Euphrates is a pixel perfect clone of the Amazon website. In it you can browse through categories of products or search for a specific item, create your own account and add them to your cart. You can also rate and review each product and calculate the total price of your purchase before you execute the transaction.

  • Backend — Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, AWS S3, BCrypt
  • Frontend — React, Redux, AJAX, Sass

Annihilate

Annihilate is a JavaScript game in which you play against an AI for the conquest of all the planets in a solar system. Colonize the galaxy before it's too late, for the aliens are eager to Annihilate our species!

  • Repo
  • Live

Shyche

Shyche - Share Your Niche, is a social media for hobbyists with unusual and specific interests such as shoe dyeing or ostrich racing. Made in collaboration with a small team of engineers, on Shyche you can create your own account, list your unique hobbies, upload videos and connect with other users through live chat, bookings and reviews.

  • Backend — Node.js, MongoDB, Express, Passport.js, Socket.io
  • Frontend — React, Redux, Axios, Sass
